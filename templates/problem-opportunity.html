Current Educational Gap

The Challenge

UFV currently lacks a dedicated program focused on AI programming and development, creating several challenges:

  • Limited access to specialized AI education
  • Students must travel to other institutions for AI-focused programs
  • Missed opportunities for local industry partnerships

Impact on Students

The absence of an AI program affects students in multiple ways:

  • Limited career opportunities in AI
  • Reduced competitiveness in the job market
  • Lack of hands-on AI project experience

The Opportunity

↓

Market Position

Position UFV as a leader in AI education in Western Canada, attracting students and industry partners.

Industry Collaboration

Create strong partnerships with tech companies and startups in the Fraser Valley region.

Economic Impact

Drive local economic growth through skilled graduates and research innovations.
